## **TypographyProvider**

`<TypographyProvider />` is responsible for passing down the theme to the `<Heading />` and `<Text />` component. It expects the theme to be of certain structure.


---


## **Heading**

Heading component grabs the theme given to `<TypographyProvider />` and applies the style defined there based on the given `color`, `sizing`, and `emphasis` prop values. It also accepts a few different non-theme related props for convenience reasons.

<Props of={Heading} />


---


## **Text**

Text component grabs the theme given to `<TypographyProvider />` and applies the style defined there based on the given `color`, `sizing`, and `emphasis` prop values. It also accepts a few different non-theme related props for convenience reasons.

<Props of={Text} />
